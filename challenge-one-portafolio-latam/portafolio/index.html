About me

Currently residing in Milan , a city celebrated for its leadership in fashion , art , and innovation , I am committed to leading interdisciplinary projects that blend science , sustainability , fashion , and   art .

My aim is to address complex and stimulating challenges by creating software solutions, while fostering meaningful collaborations between academia and industry. Through these endeavors, I aspire to promote innovative solutions and advance sustainable practices that resonate across global platforms.

Graduated in chemistry from the National Autonomous University of Mexico , formed as a Data Analyst by Google , a front-end web developer by Oracle, participant of National Projects regarding Natural products in drug discovery and recently winner of the Erasmus Mundus Joint Master Scholarship in Cheminformatics , I bring a diverse and robust background to my work.

My experience spans academic research, technology, and cross-sector partnerships, positioning me uniquely to bridge the gap between traditional industries and cutting-edge scientific advancements.
