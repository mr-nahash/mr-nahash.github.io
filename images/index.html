My Path as a Data Analyst

While studying chemistry in "Universidad Nacional Autonoma de México" (UNAM), a very quantitative field by the way. I developed a passion for solving problems through science and technology. Soon enough, I realized the power that Data Science can have; it's potential for solving complex problems, but most importantly... I discovered a new passion for data and the impact it can have in social, scientific and business matters. The possibiblities are endless!

For my surprise, Chemistry and Data Science are not very different from each other. Both use data, both create models to take decisions and both use the scientific method! (and the statistics associated with that). So my current path was not that far for my new goal...to become a Data Analyst.

Since 2020 I've been trying to solve and visualize real-world problems using real-world data to showcase the tools that I've learnt over the years. Because at the end of the day, it all comes down to crunching numbers, taking intelligent decisions and sharing to others how we got there.
